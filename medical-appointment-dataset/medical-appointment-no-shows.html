Introduction¶

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset contains some information of some patients' medical appointments and weither or not they show to their appointment.

This information is:

  • The IDs of the patient and the appointment.
  • The gendre and age of the patient.
  • The scheduled date and the appointment day.
  • Some characteristics and problems of the patients like their Scholarship(social welfare program) and weither or not they have Hypertension,Diabetes and Alcoholism and the number of Handicaps they have if any.
  • Weither the patient recived an SMS or not.
  • The location of the hospital.

  • Very few patients have Handicaps and Alcoholism while more but still few have Hipertension,Diabetes.

    week_day_dict = { 0:'Monday', 1:'Tuesday', 2:'Wednesday', 3:'Thursday', 4:'Friday', 5:'Saturday', 6:'Sunday' }
app_date =pd.to_datetime(df.AppointmentDay)
sch_date =pd.to_datetime(df.ScheduledDay)
waiting_time = (app_date-sch_date)                         
waiting_time_in_days= waiting_time.apply(lambda x:x.days+1) #one day was added to the waiting days to gurantee that if day of appointment is the same as the scheduled day then the patient waited for zero days as timing in hours of appointment day is always set to 0
app_week_day = app_date.apply(lambda x:week_day_dict[x.weekday()])

    Conclusions¶

    This notebook provides an analysis to 'the Medical Appointment No Shows' dataset to know which of the features of the patients and their appointments has a large effect on people showing/not-showing to their scheduled appointments. And as shown from the figures and analysis these strongest effective features are age,receiving an sms and the number of waiting days.

  • patients that are either too young or too old tend to show more to their appointments.
    too young -> age < 5
    too old -> age > 85

    Limitations¶

  • The dependence of showing to an appointment on age and number of waiting days is limited by some regions. We could only conclude a strong tendency to show when the patients wait less than one day but we can't conclude much for higher number of waiting days. As for age we only know that there is a tendency to show when age is less than 5 or larger than 85 but we can't conclude much if age is out of this region.

  • Giving some info about some patients and tring to predict if they show to their appointments we need to attach every thing together first so it's better to use a machine learning technique to have good predictions.
